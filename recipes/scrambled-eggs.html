Scrambled Eggs

Description

This recipe is a modification from Gordon Ramsay's delicious scrambled eggs approach.It is a quick breakfast option that won't take up too much time, around 15-20 minutes.
The trick is keep scrambling the eggs on lowish heat, this results in a lovely texture. I personally pair them with 2 toasted slices with avocado and honey for a filling breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs.
  • 15g of butter.
  • Salt to taste.
  • Black pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Melt butter on pan or skillet over medium heat.
  2. Lower the heat a bit and add the eggs.
  3. Add salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Keep those eggs moving until they begin to set.
  5. Turn off heat before they reach the desired hardness, they will keep cooking for a bit.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
